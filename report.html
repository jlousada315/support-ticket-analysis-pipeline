Executive Summary

Ticket volume surged 59% from 224 to 357 tickets between April 30 and May 1, with complaint rates climbing to 45% of total volume. Refund processing delays, product quality mismatches, and order fulfillment errors are driving dissatisfaction, with negative sentiment (41 mentions) now exceeding positive sentiment (37 mentions). This trajectory poses immediate risk to customer retention and brand reputation if operational bottlenecks aren't addressed within 48-72 hours.

Insight 2: Product Quality & Fulfillment Failures at Scale

Severity
Critical

Evidence: Product quality issues appear on both April 30 and May 1; dimension discrepancies and damaged/missing items suggest both QA gaps and handling failures in warehouse/logistics.

High
